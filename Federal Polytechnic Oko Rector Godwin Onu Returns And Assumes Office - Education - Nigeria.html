Federal Polytechnic Oko Rector Godwin Onu Returns And Assumes Office by IMMORTALSEDO: 12:44am On Dec 08, 2017
Just yesterday evening it was reported that the rector of federal polytechnic Oko Prof. Godwin Onuh who went for a short time leave have returned t0 the school to assume the office he left in the care of the governing council while he was gone.

While he came back the school, students, staffs, and other dignitaries in the school welcomes him with open hands as they moved him to the ASOROCK chambers as the rector he is.

It is being looked upon that the governing council will leave the school premises anytime soon upon the arrival of the rector.
